Cradled by beautiful green tea plantations, a historical legacy of the Sholayar Ranges, the Birla Falls is beauty is all its majesty. With water gushing down the mountain peaks, dropping from one rock to the other the Birla Falls is a beautiful waterfall encapsulated by surrounding green valleys and high peaks. The sight of the waters cascading down high peaks to flow freely along with the river is in itself a sight which fascinated you. The very first peek at the Birla Falls is enough to instigate awe in people.
